FAQ

How do I add coaches to my program?
Go to Settings and tap "Manage Coaches." Share your program invite with other coaches who have the app installed.

Does CombineIQ work without internet?
Yes. All data entry works offline. Your data syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How are player rankings calculated?
Rankings use position-weighted scoring. Each combine event is weighted based on its importance for each position. The formula is fully transparent — tap any ranking to see the breakdown.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings > your name > Subscriptions > CombineIQ.

Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored in your program's iCloud account. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information. CombineIQ is COPPA-compliant for programs with athletes under 13.
